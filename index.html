Antenna: 71 ft Random Wire

End-fed random wire antenna (~71 feet / 21.6 m) with 9:1 unun and counterpoise. Good performer across multiple HF bands with external tuner.

Typical configuration: ~71 ft wire sloper/inverted-L, fed with coax via 9:1 transformer, ~26–30 ft counterpoise or ground system.
